To the moon

The science has always been a passion of mine since I was a child. It always baffled me that we sent a man to the moon especially with the technology of the 1960s I believe it truely showed man's resilience and love for exploration. It excites me now to see how far we have come and the idea that we may very well send men and women to another planet altogether exites me even more. Technology has come a long way and though we still have a ways to go, I believe it can be achieved and I would love nothing more than to contribute to this and leave my mark as a historian and a pioneer. I think Africa is the way forward, Africa is the future

Blockchain can change the world

Blockchain has been the source of much deliberation in the past few years, with many seeing it as a get rich scheme while others see it as a source of income I however see it differently, I believe blockchain can change lives and this is a prime example of how. Blockchain is essentially a shared space allowing for far greater technological advances and greater computing power. Blockchain takes away the centralisation and gives power back to the people. In terms of technology blockchain is definately the way forward. It is the future of technology and the more it evolves and grows the better for Africa. It has the potential to solve so many real world problems especially for Africa and make it easier of people to get what they need when they need it.
